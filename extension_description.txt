CiteChatGPT - Easy Citations for ChatGPT Conversations

CiteChatGPT is a Chrome extension that allows you to quickly generate citations for your ChatGPT conversations. With just a click of a button, you can create properly formatted citations in APA, MLA, or Chicago style.

Features:
- Generate citations with a single click
- Support for APA, MLA, and Chicago citation styles
- Customizable citation format through extension options
- Automatically copies the citation to your clipboard
- Works seamlessly on the ChatGPT website

How to use:
1. Install the CiteChatGPT extension
2. Visit chat.openai.com and start a conversation with ChatGPT
3. When you want to cite the conversation, click the 'Cite' button that appears on the page
4. The citation will be automatically copied to your clipboard, ready to paste into your document

CiteChatGPT makes it easy to properly attribute information from your ChatGPT conversations in your academic or professional work. Never struggle with citing AI-generated content again!
